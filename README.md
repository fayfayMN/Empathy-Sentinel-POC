Empathy Sentinel is a Civil Tech initiative designed to act as a diagnostic support layer.

The Humanity First Principle:

Modular Architecture (The Roadmap)To manage the $9/10$ difficulty of this project, development is split into "Patches":
1. The BIFF-Analyzer (Current Phase)A lightweight tool that audits communication (emails, transcripts) for "Aggression Spikes" vs. "Informative Facts."
2. The Guardian Council ProtocolA governance layer where outputs are audited by a human-in-the-loop (HITL) panel of legal and psychological experts.
3.  Neuro-Neutral Ethics LayerA bias-mitigation engine designed to distinguish between cultural communication styles (e.g., direct vs. indirect) and manipulative behavior.

Privacy & Compliance (MCDPA / TRAIGA)We prioritize security for "New Americans" and survivors:
 Zero-Retention Policy: Data is processed in-memory and purged immediately.
 De-identification: Names and locations are "masked" before analysis to prevent re-identification.
 Explainable AI (XAI): Every "flag" is clickable, showing the user exactly which sentence triggered the alert and why.
